The truck drivers have such a strong strategic importance to the country, as we've seen in moments like 2018 Truck Drivers’ Strike that happened in Brazil, which proves that without the services of the truck drivers our country's supplies would be largely undistributed. In addition, the truck drivers have one of the worst working conditions in the country, driving for many hours in a row and with a low level of payment, forcing them to work into a lot of shifts to get enough to support their families. So, we must make sure that this workforce trusts and sees how this new technology will improve their standards of work and living in a time-intensive job. This is seen mainly in the overall trip logistics and also how sleep-deprivation and roadworks conditions affect their performance. With that in mind, in the wake of new technologies and aiming to provide a better experience to the truck drivers, our Artificial Intelligence (AI) will be equipped with a system capable of not only helping them drive, but also recognizing facial expressions, voice commands and give them better conditions while at work, further the added benefit of making safer roads for all drivers, and reducing human and monetary losses.

AI Functions

Based on the main goals mentioned, we developed an AI that will educate the user so he can apply this knowledge in other functions inside and outside the company, aware the user to contribute to a better planet, collect data from the trips and use this information to make road transport safer, faster and more efficient, while the user has a comfort and happy trip.

Benefits

With all this information and ideas, we can envision a future where truck drivers have excellent work conditions while an AI is turning him into a better and aware citizen, giving him all the information he needs to grow inside and outside the company he’s working. Besides that, with all the optimized parameters the AI has added to the information it will get while the trucks are driving, we see a reality in which the road transport is better seen, as it will be more efficient while contributing to a better society.
